Booking Road Test

Booking The Road Test

There are two parts here. Road Test Scheduling and Booking The Truck.
It is recommended to schedule The Road Test early once you started taking driving lessons.
At the same time, it will be a great idea to book the truck from the driving school to ensure availability.

Scheduling Road Test and booking a truck for it

Schedule for the Road Test
You can pay a $40 fee to the DMV and schedule the Road Test yourself or you can pay it to us and we will schedule it for you. It must be booked at least 14 days after you have received a Commercial Learner's Permit. It is highly recommended to book the Road Test through us. We know this booking system very well and use it daily. If there is a need to reschedule the test, please ensure to do so 3 days before the booking date in order not to lose the $40 fee. Once again, we also highly recommend to re-schedule through us due to our flexibility.

Book the truck for the Road Test
Once you schedule the Road Test, it is a great idea to also book the truck to ensure it is available for you. For the Road Test, we will provide the same truck you took your lessons in to ensure you are comfortable with the vehicle. The location for the Road Test at the moment is in Aqueduct, Queens. The price list is reflected on Our Pricing Page. Please contact us if you have any questions!
