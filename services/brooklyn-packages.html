Packages

CDL Packages we offer

Our packages are made as efficient as possible to ensure you are confident going to the Road Test

 Manual and Automatic Transmission Packages

  • We will enroll you in the ELDT - Mandatory Online Training.
  • We will provide you with all the necessary training materials.
  • We will train you for the Pre-Trip Inspection, the Maneuvers, and On-The-Road Driving.
  • We will schedule, pay and provide you with a truck for the Road Test(s) in Long Island.
  • We will submit your ELDT records to Federal System once your training is complete.
  • 5 hours lessons package is good for drivers with some prior experience driving trailers.
  • 10 hours package is good to obtain the most basic skills.
  • 15 hours package will get you well prepared for the Road Test.
  • 20 hours package will comprehensively prepare you for the Road Test. It consists of 15 driving lessons, 5 PTI group lessons and an extra Road Test.
  • And finally, unlimited package will give you a piece of mind with 4 months to pass the Road Test. To ensure you are well prepared, mock tests will be provided for each part of the Road Test.
