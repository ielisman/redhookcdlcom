    Commercial Learner's Permit

    Get CDL Manual at the Local DMV or Online. Learn the material. Pass the written exam and get a Commercial Learner's Permit (CLP) - valid for six months. Pass Medical examination for Interstate Driving.
    See detailed information over here.

    ELDT and Driving Lessons

    Enroll Online in Entry Level Driving Training (ELDT) or no road test admittance. Practice well for Pre-trip Inspection, Parking, Backing, Driving, etc. at our Driving School. Schedule Road Test early.
    See more information here

    The Road (Skills) Test

    Arrive early for the exam; have Non-Expired Driver's License and Commercial Learner Permit. Pass Vehicle Inspection, Basic Controls Test, and Road Test. Check with DMV for status.
