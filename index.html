    Step 1 - Commercial Permit

    Get CDL Manual at Local DMV or Online. Learn the material. Pass written exam and you will get Commercial Learner Permit (CLP) for 6 months. Pass Medical examination for Interstate Driving.
    See detailed information on that here

    Step 2 - ELDT and Driving Lessons

    Enroll Online in Entry Level Driving Training (ELDT), otherwise no road test is allowed. Practice Diligently for Pre-trip Inspection, Parking, Backing, Driving, etc. at our Driving School. Schedule Road Test early.
    See more information here

    Step 3 - CDL Road Test

    Arrive early for the exam with Non-Expired Driver License and Commercial Learner Permit. Pass Vehicle Inspection, Basic Controls Test and Road Test. Check with DMV for status.
    Read more information here
