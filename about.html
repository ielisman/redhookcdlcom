About Us

Red Hook Commercial Driving School, Inc.


We are one of the oldest Commercial Driving Schools in Brooklyn.

We opened more than 30 years ago and helped many students with their careers.

  • Generations of students got their CDL from our school
  • We are a multilingual school and speak English, Russian and Ukrainian
  • We help people from different backgrounds and cultures to achieve their goals

Our instructors are one of the best in the business; you will learn many tips and tricks from them.
They acquired it through many years of knowledge and experience. Our teachers know very well what inspectors do and ask!

That helps our students to pass their exams. Our knowledge and your determination are keys to your success!
Please do not hesitate to reach us: call us, come to our office or email us!
